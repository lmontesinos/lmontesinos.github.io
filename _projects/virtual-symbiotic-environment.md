This project explores how extended reality, digital twins, and AI-enabled modeling can support safer and more effective decision-making in complex maritime operations.

The work focuses on the integration of human, machinery, and environmental factors within immersive simulation environments. The broader objective is to support scenario exploration, training, and decision support under diverse operational conditions.

## Core components

- Virtual reality environment for maritime operational scenarios
- Human-in-the-loop simulation
- Environmental condition modeling
- Physiological and behavioral data acquisition
- Explainable AI for decision support
