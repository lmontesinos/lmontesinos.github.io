This project focuses on the environmental sustainability of operating rooms and surgical care.

The work includes evidence synthesis, analysis of grey and white literature, and the development of frameworks to improve waste management and sustainability practices in clinical environments.

## Focus

- Sustainable operating rooms
- Surgical waste management
- Environmental, social, and economic sustainability
- Grey literature and expert guidance synthesis
