My teaching and mentoring work focuses on helping engineering students develop technical competence, research skills, critical thinking, and the ability to connect theory with real-world applications.

## Teaching interests

- Biomedical signal processing
- Signals and systems
- Digital signal processing
- Data science and machine learning in biomedical engineering
- Research methodology
- Technology-enhanced and experiential learning

## Educational innovation

I am interested in the design and evaluation of active learning experiences, including:

- Challenge-based learning
- Research-based learning
- Virtual reality experiences
- AI-supported learning activities
- Hands-on signal acquisition and analysis projects

## Mentoring

I mentor undergraduate and graduate students in projects related to biomedical engineering, health data science, sustainable healthcare, wearable technologies, and extended reality.
